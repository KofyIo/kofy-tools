Line 02 — Strategic context
This product exists to deepen existing B2B coffee accounts — not to open new logistics routes. Every café already buying Kofy coffee has a cold-chain problem. The milk sachet solves it and arrives in the same delivery. One relationship. Multiple SKUs. One route. The goal is to become the complete specialty supply partner for every account — beans, milk, and what comes next.
